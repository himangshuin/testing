Who is Himu?

Best Astrologer in Guwahati & One of India's Top Spiritual Guides

Himu, the founder of Astro Himu, is widely recognized as the best astrologer in Guwahati and one of the most sought-after spiritual consultants in India. Based in Krishnanagar, Guwahati, Assam, Himu has guided 500+ clients across India — helping them navigate love, career, health, finance, and life purpose through the sacred sciences of Vedic Astrology, Tarot, Numerology, and Vastu.

Himu's journey into the cosmic sciences began with a deep personal calling — a desire to understand the patterns woven into every life through the language of the stars, numbers, and cards. Over the years, Himu has trained rigorously in Vedic Jyotish (Vedic Astrology), earning certifications that reflect both traditional mastery and modern interpretive skill. Clients consistently describe Himu's readings as uncannily accurate, compassionate, and genuinely life-changing.

What makes Himu stand apart as the best astrologer in India is the holistic approach — combining Kundali analysis, planetary remedies, Tarot intuition, and Numerology insights into a single, powerful consultation. This multi-layered method ensures that no matter what challenge you bring — a career crossroads, relationship confusion, health concerns, or a business decision — you leave with clear, actionable cosmic guidance.
