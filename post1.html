What Is a Tarot Birth Card?

A Tarot Birth Card is a Major Arcana card that corresponds to your date of birth. Unlike your daily card pulls or spread readings, your Birth Card is permanent — it represents the core energy you came into this world with and the soul lessons you are here to master.

In my practice as one of the leading Tarot readers in Guwahati and across India, I use Birth Card readings as the foundation of any deep spiritual consultation. It gives remarkable clarity about a person's core personality, purpose, and patterns — things that often shock clients with their accuracy.

How to Calculate Your Tarot Birth Card

The calculation is rooted in numerology. Here's the simple method:

1
Write Your Full Birth Date
Example: 15 / 08 / 1990 → write as 1+5+0+8+1+9+9+0 = 33
2
Reduce to a Number 1–22
33 → 3+3 = 6. That's Card VI — The Lovers.
3
Find Your Card in the Major Arcana
Numbers 1-22 correspond directly to Major Arcana cards (22 = 0, The Fool).
