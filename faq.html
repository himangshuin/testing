Vedic Astrology & Kundali
What is Vedic Astrology and how is it different from Western Astrology?

Vedic Astrology (Jyotish) is the ancient Indian system of astrology based on the sidereal zodiac — the actual positions of stars. Western astrology uses the tropical zodiac, which is fixed to the seasons. Vedic astrology is considered more precise for prediction and includes the Dasha (planetary period) system, which Western astrology does not use. Himu practices both but specializes in Vedic Jyotish for in-depth life guidance.

What information do I need for a Kundali reading?

For an accurate Kundali (birth chart) reading, you'll need your: Date of birth, Exact time of birth (as precise as possible — even 1–2 minutes matters), and Place of birth (city/town). If you don't know your exact birth time, Himu can work with approximate times or use Tarot as a complementary tool.

Can astrology really predict the future?

Vedic Astrology identifies planetary periods (Dashas), transits, and chart patterns to indicate likely trends, favorable timings, and areas of life that need attention. It's less about predicting a fixed future and more about helping you understand your cosmic blueprint — so you can make better, more informed choices. Himu uses astrology as a tool for empowerment, not fear.

What are astrological remedies and do they work?

Astrological remedies are prescribed actions — such as wearing specific gemstones, performing rituals (pujas), mantra chanting, fasting on certain days, or charity — designed to strengthen benefic planets and reduce malefic influences in your chart. Himu recommends practical, accessible remedies and many clients report positive shifts after following them consistently.

Tarot · Numerology · Vastu
How is Tarot different from Astrology?

Astrology works with your birth chart and planetary cycles to reveal life patterns and timing. Tarot uses 78 symbolic cards to access intuitive insight on specific questions — love, career, decisions, hidden influences. Himu frequently uses Tarot alongside Astrology for a richer, more holistic reading. Tarot works well when you have a focused question in mind.

What can Numerology reveal about me?

Numerology uses your birth date and name to calculate core numbers — Life Path, Expression, Soul Urge, Destiny, and Personal Year numbers — that reveal your innate talents, personality, karmic lessons, and life cycles. It's particularly useful for business naming, relationship compatibility, and understanding why certain years feel harder or easier than others.

What is Vastu Shastra and how can it help my home or business?

Vastu Shastra is the ancient Indian science of architecture and spatial energy. It aligns the layout and directions of your home or office with natural energy flows to attract peace, prosperity, health, and success. Himu's Vastu consultations assess your floor plan and provide directional recommendations and energy-correction remedies — many of which don't require demolition or major renovations.
